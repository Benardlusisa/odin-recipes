French beans

This is a plate of french beans

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • galic
  • salt
  • french
  • cooking oil
  • green grams

procedure for cooking

  1. Boil the green grams properly and french beans
  2. Wash the onions and tomatoes
  3. Carefully cut the onions in small pieces
  4. Cut the tomatoes also in small pieces
  5. Put the cooking pan on the source of heat and smear cooking oil on it
  6. Wait it to gain heat and place the pieces of onions on the cooking oil
  7. Allow them to cook until they are golden brown
  8. Now place the tomatoes and mix them properly
  9. Mix them with the fried the boiled green gramsand french beans
  10. Allow them to cook for ten minutes and serve it while hot
