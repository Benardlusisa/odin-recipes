Baked kale chips

This is a baked kale chips

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • galic
  • salt
  • pilled potatoes
  • cooking oil
  • green grams

procedure for cooking

  1. Boil the green grams properly
  2. Cut the potatoes in pieces and fry them in oil
  3. Wash the onions and tomatoes
  4. Carefully cut the onions in small pieces
  5. Cut the tomatoes also in small pieces
  6. Put the cooking pan on the source of heat and smear cooking oil on it
  7. Wait it to gain heat and place the pieces of onions on the cooking oil
  8. Allow them to cook until they are golden brown
  9. Now place the tomatoes and mix them properly
  10. Mix them with the fried potatoes and the boiled green grams
  11. Allow them to cook for ten minutes and serve it while hot
