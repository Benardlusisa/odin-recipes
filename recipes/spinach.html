Stew

This is a plate of stew

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Galic
  • Salt
  • Potatoes
  • Meat
  • Carrots
  • Cooking oil
  • Green grams

procedure for cooking

  1. Boil the green grams properly and meat
  2. Wash the onions, tomatoes, and carrots
  3. Carefully cut the onions in small pieces
  4. Cut the tomatoes also in small pieces and carrots
  5. Put the cooking pan on the source of heat and smear cooking oil on it
  6. Wait it to gain heat and place the pieces of onions on the cooking oil
  7. Allow them to cook until they are golden brown
  8. Now place the tomatoes and carrots and mix them properly
  9. Allow them to cook for five minutes while stiring
  10. Add the poatoes and mix properly
  11. Add the green grams and meat and mix them properly
  12. Allow them to cook for thirty minutes and serve it while hot
